A script for Roblox Waterloo at Home that optimizes quality of life, allowing to reload cannons with a keybind and no animation, as well as chop trees without having to spam click, as well as other useful features to enhance your Water torture experience.

Press B/N to throw grenade far/high.

Equip Axe and press H for chopping trees. (You might want to press it again to toggle it off after like 3-5 seconds as it will lag up the client) [Note: Not recommended in public servers]

V for reloading/cleaning/worming cannons (press again and again after like 2 seconds to do the next step) [Note: Be careful, as this might affect nearby enemy cannons]

M for attacking destructible buildings within 125 studs. Must have melee equipped. (anything you can melee, forts too!)
