A script for Roblox Waterloo at Home that optimizes quality of life, allowing to reload cannons with a keybind and no animation, as well as chop trees without having to spam click.

Press B/N to throw grenade high/low. (Must be in Slot 3)

Equip Axe and press H for chopping trees. (You might want to press it again to toggle it off after like 3-5 seconds as it will lag up the client)

V for reloading/cleaning/worming cannons (press again and again after like 2 seconds to do the next step)
